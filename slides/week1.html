Course Aims

  • make students aware of effects of experimental design
  • good understanding of technologies and methods for Bioinformatics
  • introduce basic coding in R

Course Aims - specifics

  • Walk through RNA-seq workflow in R
    • quality assessment
    • differential expression
    • visualisation
    • downstream analysis
  • By the end you should have a reproducible report for the above analysis

Course Aims - high-level

  • Creating reproducible reports
    • not just showing the answer - but how you got to it
  • Getting stuck, and figuring out how to solve errors
  • Interpreting and questioning data
